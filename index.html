Biography & Core Mission

I am Rahul Kushwaha, a B.Tech student studying at United Institute of Technology (United Group of Institutions), technology developer, web engineer, mobile architect, and independent researcher dedicated to exploring the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Educational Technology (EdTech), Cognitive Physiology, and Human Psychology.

My core mission as a student developer is to design adaptive learning platforms that replace passive content scrolling with active, scenario-based decision-making.
